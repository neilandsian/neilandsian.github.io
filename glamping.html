> Glamping Pods

The site has 10 glamping pods, perfect for families or groups. Each pod can sleep up to 5 people, although this involves sharing a large communal bed, so really it'll work for a single family or two very open couples

Note: Bedding and towels are not provided by the venue, but we have some spare sets available if you’re traveling from afar or by train. Just let us know in advance if you need to borrow some.

Cost: £200 for the weekend (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday nights).


> Features

  • Lighting, heating, and an electric point (included in the price)
  • Fridge, kettle, and microwave
  • Beds sleeping up to 5 (don’t forget your own bedding and towels)
  • Private decking area with a fire/BBQ pit
  • Outside water tap
  • Designated parking space

> Shared Facilities at the Stable

The Stable offers extra amenities to make your stay comfortable:

  • Laundry facilities and a washing-up area
  • 4 family-sized bathrooms, including one with wheelchair access

All bathrooms are heated and include:

  • Shower, sink, and toilet
  • Baby changing facilities
