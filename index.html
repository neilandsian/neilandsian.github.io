> Plan for the Weekend

Get ready for a weekend filled with festivities! Here’s the quick rundown:

  • Friday Night: BBQ for everyone.
  • Saturday: Ceremony at 12:30 PM, followed by food, live music, and evening refreshments.
  • Sunday: Breakfast for everyone, with pizza for those staying the night.

> Daily Details

Friday Night

The festivities kick off with a relaxed BBQ for everyone to mingle, catch up, and settle in. Fire up your appetite—this is just the start of the weekend!

Saturday

The main event! The ceremony starts at 12:30 PM, followed by a feast and celebrations. We’ll have live music, so bring your dancing shoes, and there will be plenty of refreshments in the evening to keep the party going.

Sunday

We’ll start the day with breakfast for everyone to recharge. For those who want to stretch the weekend a bit further, pizza will be served in the evening for anyone staying the night. Feel free to relax and enjoy—there’s no rush to leave!
