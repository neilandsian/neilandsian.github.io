Accommodation

We’ve got a range of options to make your stay as enjoyable as possible, whether you’re looking for maximum comfort or a bit of adventure.

The best choice for your stay: Our **Glamping Pods**! These are onsite, fully heated, and available for everyone to book. With limited availability (only 10 pods, first come, first served), they’re the perfect cozy retreat for the weekend.

If the pods fill up, don’t worry—we’ve got backup options, including Bell Tents or the flexibility to bring your own setup:

  • Glamping Pods (10 available, First Come, First Served): £200 for the weekend. More Info
  • Bell Tents (for when pods are booked): £200 for the weekend. More Info
  • Bring Your Own Tent, Campervan, or Caravan: Free!

Please book your Glamping Pod as soon as possible to guarantee availability!

All options include access to onsite showers and toilets, so you can enjoy some home comforts while you’re here. And the best part? You don’t have to leave until Monday!
